Founder of the Smile Clinic

THE SMILE CLINIC

Where we care about your smile

The Smile Clinic was founded in 1989 by Serge Frejlich. Doctor Frejlich is an all-around dentist, specialized in prosthodontics; also known as esthetic dentistry.

The Smile Clinic has two locations with a total of 5 treatment rooms, easy accessibility and ample parking options. We work solely with renowned dental labs, outstanding dental professionals and contemporary equipment which means we can guarantee excellent quality.

Our clinics are spacious and modern. We believe it is important to evolve with the technological possibilities in our field and we annually invest in new and improved equipment!
